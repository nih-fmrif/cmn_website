Data Science and Sharing Team

What we do

The Data Science and Sharing Team supports investigators within the NIMH intramural program in creating, distributing, and leveraging large, open datasets to accelerate discovery. We provide tools and training to help the IRP embrace open science and stay on the cutting edge of novel datasets, tools, and platforms. You can find a summary of our activities over the last year in our annual report.
